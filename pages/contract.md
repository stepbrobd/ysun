I take on contract work selectively, when the scope is interesting and there is
no conflict of interest (mostly Nix/NixOS, networking, and performance
engineering). Rates are in EUR per hour, exclusive of tax; VAT is added where
applicable. Work is time and materials, billed in 30-minute increments with a
4-hour minimum per engagement. Compute and expenses are billed separately, and
any pre-existing tooling I bring remains mine.

- 50/h (open). Everything I write is GPLv3 from the outset, and I retain the
  copyright. GPLv3 already permits you to run, modify, redistribute, and resell
  the work, provided you make the source available under the same terms.
  Provided as-is, without warranty.

- 125/h (private). The work remains confidential under NDA and is not published,
  and all IP is assigned to you upon payment. Provided as-is, with a narrow
  originality and non-infringement warranty available on request.

The first hour is free, to discuss scope and confirm fit. If it works, reach out
(contact details on the [home page](/)) and we can take it from there.
